Weapon Stat Calculator

GUI calculator for the uploaded weapon stat data. Pick a weapon family and frame/exotic, enter stat values, and the page calculates damage, crit damage, burst totals, falloff, reload, handling, magazine size, and PvE combatant scaling.

Inputs

Formula assumptions
Linear/quadratic stats use evpp × stat² + vpp × stat + offset. Crit damage uses base × (1 + crit_mult / 100). PvE damage uses pve_damage × weapon_pve × extra_pve × combatant_scalar. Burst total multiplies per-shot damage by burst_size. DPS estimates use the delay fields as 30 FPS frames.
